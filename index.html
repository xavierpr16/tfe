Travel for Everyone

The Problem:

Make the process of booking a trip much easier for young and older professionals with busy lives.

The Solution:

With the app TFE it would focus on the most attractive destinations and how to make it the smoothest experience based on they're history of previous trips and monetary budgets. While featuring many tools to navigate their destinations much easier. 

Definition & Ideation

User Statement

Jason needs help navigating the airports in which he departs and lands. Jason has had trouble in the past because of complicated maps at the airport.

Problem Statement

Jason needs better navigation and budgeting tips in order to accomplish a better overall user experience when traveling through the airports. Value Proposition

My organization TFE is developing better budgeting and navigational tools to help young and older users to solve the lack of initiative to help users get through airports and to their destinations. We’re better because our competitors might help highlight the best deals but not help with the process of navigating the journey and taking their budget into consideration. We’re believable because we will work with airports and other travel agencies to have the most precise information.
